---
description: Creates an issue from draft materials
---
Take all materials from $ARGUMENTS and create a new issue in @hugo/content/issues/ . First should be the content from draft, after that the summary of links. Do not change anything in the content, just collect it all into a new hugo page.

The issue name should be in format of {current_year}-{next number of the issue for this year}. So look at existing issues and figure out the proper number.
